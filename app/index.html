Makenzie + Vance

Makenzie and Vance met in San Diego in 2011, bonding over a shared love of craft beer at O’Brien’s Pub. Since then they’ve moved together up the California coast through Los Angeles before settling in Oakland. Along the way they’ve supported each other through many adventures—long bike rides, travels abroad, new jobs, new friends—and now they’re ready to publicly acknowledge the commitment they’ve made to build a life together, in the presence of their close friends and family. He’s “kind, funny, smart” (according to Makenzie); she’s “fiesty, goofy, and roughhouses like an MMA fighter” (according to Vance and, well, everyone). Together, they’re unstoppable!

Martin Johnson House

The ceremony and brunch reception will gather our friends and family under the summer sun (weather permitting) at the Martin Johnson House. This cozy, historic cottage is nestled among the cypress trees on the coastal bluffs of La Jolla, California, with sweeping views of the Pacific Ocean.
