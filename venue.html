Venue

Martin Johnson House

The ceremony and dinner reception will gather our friends and family before the setting sun at the Martin Johnson House. This cozy, historic cottage is nestled among the cypress trees on the coastal bluffs of La Jolla, California, with sweeping views of the Pacific Ocean.

The ceremony and reception will both take place outdoors so please dress appropriately for a breezy (sometimes chilly) San Diego summer evening. Flat soled shoes are also recommended since the grounds are mostly grass.

Ample parking will be available at the venue if you choose to drive. Biking is recommended but not required!
